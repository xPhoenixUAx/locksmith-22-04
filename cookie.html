Cookie Policy

This policy explains how the website operated under uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What cookies are used for

Cookies help the site remember functional preferences, maintain basic navigation state, and measure how visitors use pages so the user experience can be improved over time. Essential cookies may support security, session continuity, and form-related interactions. Optional cookies may support traffic analytics or conversion measurement.

2. Types of cookies

Essential cookies are required for core site operation. Analytics cookies help measure page performance, device behavior, and engagement with service pages and contact actions. Marketing cookies, if enabled in future implementations, may be used to understand campaign effectiveness across channels.

3. Third-party services

If analytics, maps, call tracking, scheduling tools, or embedded media are added to the website, those services may place their own cookies subject to their respective policies. Examples can include analytics services, call measurement systems, CRM forms, or customer messaging widgets.

4. Managing preferences

Visitors can control or delete cookies through browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect the operation of forms, embedded content, or convenience features. Where legally required, a consent banner should be configured to allow users to manage non-essential cookies before they are activated.

Privacy Policy

This privacy notice explains how collects, uses, and protects information provided through this website.

1. Information collected

The website may collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, property or vehicle details, service request descriptions, and technical usage data such as browser type, referral information, and page interactions. Information is collected when visitors complete forms, call, email, or interact with connected analytics tools.

2. How information is used

Information is used to respond to request inquiries, communicate about availability or scheduling, improve website performance, and maintain internal service records. Where permitted, information may also be used to evaluate advertising or campaign effectiveness.

3. Sharing of information

Information is not sold. It may be shared with service providers that support website hosting, analytics, communication tools, payment handling, scheduling, or CRM systems where such sharing is necessary to operate the business. Information may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect the company, its users, or the public.

4. Data retention

Inquiry and service information may be retained for operational, legal, tax, fraud prevention, warranty, and customer support purposes for as long as reasonably necessary. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of service request and governing legal obligations.

5. Security measures

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect personal information. However, no online transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and users should avoid sending sensitive credentials or entry codes through unsecured channels.
